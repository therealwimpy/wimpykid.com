Company Internship

Melham Construction Corporation

MELHAM CONSTRUCTION CORPORATION (MCC), first registered on February 2020 in the Philippine government situated at no.27 commonwealth avenue Diliman north Fairview Quezon City. The main goal is to cater the growing demand of industrialization of the country. Melham Construction Corporation (MCC) cater broader scope dealing with vertical project such (Structural, Fit out works), Horizontal Project such (Roads, Highways and bridges), Telecommunication such (Cell site erection) and Technology such (Installation of fiber optics, SLI and ODN).

Unified Internship Program

Unified Internship Program (UIP) are consist of various companies such but not limited to Melham Construction Corporation, Anafara Products Trading, Joje Philippines, Tinta Beauty Products, Visvis travel and tours, and many more. We focus on cultivating and sustaining a diverse internship environment and culture and maximize interns potential by expanding professional opportunities through learning and development - "Work from Home Internship Program".
